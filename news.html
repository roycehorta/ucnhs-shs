Make a Change, Start taking Action, and Help Save the Earth. Let’s start the discipline within ourselves, and let's unite to save our one and only home.

It is time to take an action and let our voices be heard. Scientists are now in an outcry, desperately protesting and warning the public about the current global climate crisis, claiming that we only have 4-5 years to save the planet. As student leaders, we are here to spread awareness and empower the youth to open their eyes to the reality, and start moving to save our planet.

Let’s start the discipline within ourselves, and let’s unite to save our one and only home. The officers of the SSG Organisation made posters, comics, and infographics to help spread awareness and educate other students with this matter. UCNHS SSG Is One with the Environment!
