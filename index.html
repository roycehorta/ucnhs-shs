FOREWORD

The Urdaneta City National High School holds its legacy as a hallmark institution that provides quality education to every Filipino learner who enters its thresholds. With pride and honor, the institution continues to uphold its tenets, goals,mission and vision that have already made its mark in history by producing leaders, teachers, and game-changers of society.

For many decades, Urdaneta City National High School continues to grow and excel as a foundation of knowledge by honing learners and teachers holistically. As an academic institution,it also continues to expand its facilities, programs and initiatives reaching the unreached and marginalized learners to provide not only accessible education but also equity in learning and teaching opportunities.

The implementation of the Senior High School program as part of the Department of Education’s K to 12 Curriculum, the institution paved way for the ample opportunities for growth and development both for educators and learners. The institution offered various strands- ABM, Arts and Design, GAS, HUMSS, STEM and Tech-Voc, to cater the different needs,interests, and intelligences of the learners to hone their utmost potentials and capabilities.
